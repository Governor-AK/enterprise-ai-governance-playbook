# AI Risk Register Template

## Risk Register Record

> **Important**
>
> The Enterprise AI Risk Register is a living governance record.
>
> While this template presents the complete enterprise risk record, individual fields are progressively completed as governance activities are performed throughout the AI Governance lifecycle.
>
> Each field identifies the governance capability responsible for completing or updating the information.
