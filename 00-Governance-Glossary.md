# Enterprise AI Governance Glossary

## Purpose

This glossary establishes the canonical terminology used throughout the Enterprise AI Governance Playbook.

It provides a single authoritative source for governance terminology, stakeholder definitions, accountability concepts, governance records, lifecycle terminology, and decision authority.

Every capability, artifact, template, register, report, and framework alignment within this repository must use the terminology defined here.

---

# Governance Principles

The following principles apply throughout this repository.

- Every governance concept has one authoritative owner.
- Every governance record has one authoritative source.
- Summaries never replace authoritative records.
- Governance activities must be traceable across the lifecycle.
- Governance responsibilities and decision authority must remain clearly separated.
- Reusable templates support implementation but do not define governance.
- Where terminology conflicts exist, this glossary takes precedence.

---

# Canonical Stakeholder Set

The Enterprise AI Governance Playbook uses the following stakeholder model.

- Executive Sponsor
- Mortgage Operations
- Mortgage Processing Team
- AI Governance Lead
- AI Platform Team
- Data & AI Engineering
- Human-in-the-Loop Specialists
- Quality Assurance
- Risk Management
- Privacy Office
- Information Security
- Legal & Compliance
- Procurement / Third-Party Management
- Internal Audit

External AI providers, cloud providers, OCR providers, technology vendors, and service providers are treated as external governance participants rather than internal governance authorities.
