# Enterprise Third-Party AI Register Template

> **Important**
>
> The Enterprise Third-Party AI Register is a living governance record.
>
> This template presents the complete lifecycle record for a material external AI relationship. Individual fields are progressively completed or updated only when the governance activity responsible for that information has occurred.
>
> Detailed assessments, contracts, evidence, incidents, and change records remain within their authoritative source artifacts. The register records their approved outcomes, current status, and references.
