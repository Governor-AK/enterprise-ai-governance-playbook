# AI Assurance

## Executive Summary

AI Controls establish the governance measures intended to manage prioritized AI risks. AI Assurance provides independent evaluation of those controls to determine whether they have been appropriately designed, implemented, and operated throughout the defined assurance period.

Building upon the AI Controls capability, Megastar Mortgage performs assurance planning, evaluates approved AI controls, documents assurance findings, records management's corrective-action commitments, and communicates an overall assurance conclusion supported by objective evidence.

Rather than creating separate governance records, AI Assurance provides verified assurance outputs that are transferred through established governance processes to the Enterprise AI Control Register and Enterprise AI Risk Register, preserving authoritative records while maintaining assurance independence.

The outputs produced within this capability provide objective confidence in the organization's AI control environment and support ongoing governance oversight.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the AI Assurance capability is to establish a standardized approach for independently evaluating AI governance controls throughout the Enterprise AI Governance Program.

This capability plans assurance activities, evaluates approved AI controls, documents assurance findings, records management's corrective-action commitments, and communicates evidence-based assurance conclusions.

Completion of this capability provides the independent assurance required to support ongoing Continuous Monitoring and future governance decisions.

---

## Governance Approach

Every governed AI system follows a consistent AI assurance process.

Assurance planning, control testing, findings evaluation, corrective-action planning, and assurance reporting are performed as one integrated assurance engagement.

The resulting assurance evidence, conclusions, and management commitments support controlled updates to the Enterprise AI Control Register and Enterprise AI Risk Register while preserving those registers as the organization's authoritative governance records.

Management visibility is provided through an AI Assurance Summary that consolidates the overall assurance conclusion without duplicating detailed testing records, findings, or register information.

## Why This Capability Matters

Implementing governance controls does not, by itself, demonstrate that they achieve their intended objectives.

AI Assurance provides an independent evaluation of approved controls using objective evidence, structured testing, and consistent assurance practices. This enables governance leaders to understand whether controls continue to provide the intended level of governance confidence and where improvement is required.

This capability transforms assurance from isolated control testing into an integrated governance evaluation process.

---

## Framework Alignment

The activities performed within this capability support internationally recognized governance and assurance practices, including:

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (Govern, Measure, Manage)
- ISO/IEC 42001 – Artificial Intelligence Management System
- ISO 19011 – Guidelines for Auditing Management Systems
- The Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA) International Standards
- COSO Internal Control Framework

Rather than reproducing framework requirements, this capability demonstrates how AI governance controls are independently evaluated within an operational governance program.
