# Monitoring Findings & Escalation Template

> **Important**
>
> A monitoring observation is not automatically a formal finding.
>
> This template validates the observation, determines materiality, assigns disposition, establishes a finding where required, defines corrective actions and escalation, records specialist-governance handoffs, and supports verified closure.
>
> The template does not replace authoritative risk, control, assurance, provider, incident, change, or governance-decision records.
