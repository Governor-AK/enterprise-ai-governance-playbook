# AI Governance Dashboard Template

> **Important**
>
> The AI Governance Dashboard is a presentation layer.
>
> Every displayed metric, KPI, KRI, target, tolerance, threshold, control-health result, trend, and status must originate from an approved governance artifact or authoritative record.
>
> The dashboard shall not redefine metric formulas, risk ratings, control-effectiveness conclusions, incident classifications, change decisions, provider conclusions, or residual-risk decisions.
