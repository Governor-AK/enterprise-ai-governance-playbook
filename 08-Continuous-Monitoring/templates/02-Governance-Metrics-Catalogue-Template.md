# Governance Metrics Catalogue Template

> **Important**
>
> The Governance Metrics Catalogue is the authoritative reference for approved AI governance measures used within Continuous Monitoring.
>
> A metric shall be entered into the catalogue only when its purpose, definition, source, calculation, ownership, quality requirements, interpretation, and status have been documented.
>
> Formal KPI and KRI design, including targets, tolerances, thresholds, and escalation rules, is completed through the KPI & KRI Framework.
