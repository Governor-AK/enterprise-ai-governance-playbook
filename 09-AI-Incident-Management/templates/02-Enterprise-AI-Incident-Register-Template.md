# Enterprise AI Incident Register Template

> **Important**
>
> The Enterprise AI Incident Register is the authoritative current-state record for formal AI incidents.
>
> Use identifiers and references to connect detailed intake, severity, response, investigation, root-cause, corrective-action, notification, verification, and closure records. Do not copy complete narratives or evidence into the register.
