# Executive Summary

Enterprise AI governance requires structured forums where governance activities are reviewed, decisions are discussed, risks are escalated, and stakeholders remain aligned.

Rather than relying on ad hoc discussions, Megastar Mortgage establishes formal governance forums to support consistent oversight of the Megastar Intelligent Processor (MIP).

These forums provide a structured mechanism for governance coordination, decision support, performance review, issue escalation, and continual governance improvement.

---

# Objective

The objective of this document is to establish the governance forums that support enterprise AI governance throughout the lifecycle of MIP.

Each forum has a defined purpose, participants, meeting frequency, and expected outcomes to ensure governance activities remain coordinated and transparent.

# Forum Responsibilities

Governance forums support the organization by:

- Reviewing governance performance.
- Monitoring implementation progress.
- Discussing significant governance issues.
- Reviewing operational trends.
- Escalating matters requiring executive attention.
- Supporting informed governance decisions.
- Promoting cross-functional collaboration.

---

# Escalation Path

Governance issues are resolved at the lowest appropriate level whenever possible.

Where additional oversight is required, issues are escalated through progressively higher governance forums until an appropriate governance decision is reached.

This structured escalation approach promotes timely resolution while maintaining executive visibility for significant governance matters.

---

# Governance Principles

Governance forums operate according to the following principles:

- Appropriate stakeholder participation.
- Transparent discussion.
- Evidence-based decision support.
- Timely escalation.
- Documented outcomes.
- Continuous organizational learning.

---

# Meeting Outputs

Each governance forum should produce appropriate governance records, including:

- Meeting agenda.
- Attendance record.
- Key discussion points.
- Decisions reached.
- Action items.
- Assigned owners.
- Target completion dates.

These records contribute to governance transparency and organizational accountability.

---

# Why This Document Matters

Enterprise AI governance depends upon structured communication and coordinated oversight.

Governance forums provide the organizational mechanism through which stakeholders collaborate, governance issues are reviewed, decisions are supported, and continual governance improvement is enabled.

Without formal governance forums, governance activities become fragmented and organizational accountability becomes difficult to maintain.
