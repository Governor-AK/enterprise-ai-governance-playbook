# Purpose

This document establishes the governance forums through which Megastar Mortgage oversees the governance of MIP.

It defines:

- governance forums;
- forum purpose;
- forum ownership;
- membership;
- meeting cadence;
- governance inputs;
- governance outputs;
- escalation pathways;
- governance record requirements.

The document establishes how governance issues move through the organization without redefining decision authority or operational responsibilities.

---

# Governance Forum Principles

Governance forums exist to ensure that governance remains structured, transparent, and evidence-based.

Each forum should:

- review information appropriate to its authority;
- make or support governance decisions within delegated authority;
- document outcomes;
- assign actions;
- monitor completion of actions;
- escalate unresolved matters when required.

Forums coordinate governance activities. They do not replace accountable business ownership.

---

# Escalation Principles

Governance matters should be resolved at the lowest level with appropriate authority.

Escalation is required when:

- delegated authority is exceeded;
- material risk remains unresolved;
- specialist functions disagree;
- legal, privacy, or security concerns cannot be resolved operationally;
- customer impact may be significant;
- regulatory obligations may be affected;
- corrective actions are ineffective or overdue;
- continued operation of MIP becomes uncertain.

Escalation should preserve traceability and identify the accountable decision authority.

---

# Governance Records

Each governance forum should maintain records appropriate to its activities, including:

- agenda;
- attendance;
- reviewed evidence;
- decisions;
- action items;
- accountable owners;
- target dates;
- escalation decisions;
- closure status.

These records support governance transparency and future assurance activities.
