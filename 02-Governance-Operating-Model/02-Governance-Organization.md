# Executive Summary

Effective AI governance requires a governance structure that brings together business, technology, and oversight functions under a common operating model.

Rather than assigning governance to a single department, Megastar Mortgage establishes a cross-functional governance organization where each stakeholder contributes expertise while remaining accountable for their respective responsibilities.

This document defines the governance organization responsible for overseeing the implementation, operation, monitoring, and continuous improvement of the Megastar Intelligent Processor (MIP).

---

# Organizational Objective

The objective of the Governance Organization is to establish a clear governance structure that supports enterprise AI adoption through coordinated oversight, defined accountability, and collaborative decision-making.

The governance organization ensures that AI governance is embedded into business operations rather than operating as a separate compliance function.

---

# Governance Structure

The governance organization consists of four governance layers.

| Governance Layer | Purpose |
|------------------|---------|
| Executive Oversight | Provides strategic direction, sponsorship, and executive accountability. |
| Business Operations | Owns business processes and operational outcomes supported by AI. |
| Technology & AI | Designs, maintains, and supports enterprise AI capabilities. |
| Governance & Assurance | Provides governance oversight, risk management, compliance, privacy, security, and independent assurance. |

Together, these governance layers establish the organizational foundation for enterprise AI governance.

# Governance Collaboration

The Governance Organization operates through collaboration rather than hierarchy.

Business functions define operational objectives.

Technology teams implement and maintain AI capabilities.

Governance functions establish oversight and accountability.

Risk, Privacy, Security, and Legal & Compliance provide independent review and challenge.

Internal Audit evaluates governance effectiveness independently.

---

# Governance Responsibilities

The Governance Organization is responsible for:

- Coordinating enterprise AI governance activities.
- Supporting responsible AI adoption.
- Providing governance oversight throughout the AI lifecycle.
- Facilitating cross-functional collaboration.
- Escalating governance matters where appropriate.
- Supporting governance reporting and management review.

Detailed ownership of individual responsibilities is established within the Roles & Responsibilities artifact.

# Organizational Principles

The Governance Organization operates according to the following principles:

- Shared accountability.
- Clear governance ownership.
- Cross-functional collaboration.
- Independent oversight.
- Transparent governance.
- Continuous organizational improvement.
